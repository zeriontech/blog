<p>NFTs are more than just JPEGs.</p><p>Some nonfungible tokens have utility far beyond aesthetics. Holding such NFTs grants the wallet owner certain rights. After that, Zerion displays everything you hold in your wallet, including tokens staked across over 500 DeFi protocols. You can review your trading history, your entry and exit points, automatically calculate your profits.</p><p>And this is not limited to just your crypto wallet: you can check any address and see what it holds. You can even subscribe to follow the wallets of funds and whales and get notifications when they trade.</p><p>In summer 2021, Zerion expanded beyond DeFi and added a new NFT tab.</p><p>In just a few clicks, you can see all your NFTs, track floor and latest prices. It’s similar to how different people have unique perspectives on the same product and the broader Web3 space. Some focus on DeFi, others are obsessed with NFTs, while many experiment with DAOs and other new frontiers. The lines between finance, creativity, and identity are getting blurred as we all collaboratively build Web3.</p><p>And to creatively express this vision, Zerion worked with a fascinating “artist”, Nikolay Ironov.</p><h2 id="who-is-nikolay-ironov">Who is Nikolay Ironov?</h2><p>Nikolay Ironov is not actually a human — it’s an advanced AI developed by Art. Lebedev.</p><p>This AI was introduced to clients as a designer working remotely. Named Nikolay Ironov, the AI has its own profile and a design portfolio. This ensured that AI’s output would be judged without any prejudice against generative design. Only a select few Art Lebedev employees knew the truth.</p><p>For a whole year, Nikolay Ironov worked on real client projects, designing logos and corporate brands.
