<p>Polymarket has quickly become the go-to platform for trading on real-world events. This guide breaks down how it works, how to trade your first market, and how Zerion helps you track every position effortlessly.</p><h2 id="what-is-polymarket">What is Polymarket?</h2><p><a href="https://polymarket.com/" rel="noreferrer">Polymarket</a> is a leading prediction market platform where you can trade on real-world events.</p><p>Instead of guessing outcomes or arguing online, Polymarket gives you a way to put money behind your beliefs. Polymarket covers everything from politics and sports to crypto, global events, and pop culture. Each market is structured around a simple yes-or-no question, and the prices represent the probability the crowd assigns to that outcome.</p><h3 id="how-polymarket-works">How Polymarket works</h3><p>Polymarket operates using "shares" that represent either side of a prediction.&nbsp;</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-11-20-at-11.41.06.png" class="kg-image" alt="screenshot of Polymarket" loading="lazy" width="2000" height="778" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-11-20-at-11.41.06.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1000/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-11-20-at-11.41.06.png 1000w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1600/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-11-20-at-11.41.06.png 1600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w2400/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-11-20-at-11.41.06.png 2400w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"><figcaption><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">Buying YES or NO for any event on Polymarket</span></figcaption></figure><p>If a market asks, “Will BTC hit $150k in 2025?” you can buy YES or NO shares. Prices move based on demand, and your profit comes from buying low and selling high — or holding until the market resolves. When the outcome is known, winning shares settle at $1 while losing shares resolve to $0. This way, the prices are probabilities: if a YES costs $0.50 it’s effectively a 50% chance of something happening.&nbsp;</p><p>Behind the scenes, Polymarket runs onchain infrastructure on Polygon, ensuring transparency, verifiability, and trustless settlement. Traders trade with each other using a limit order book and each market lives onchain. All prices are settled in USDC on Polygon.&nbsp;</p><p>The best part is that Polymarket doesn’t currently charge any fee. They even cover gas fees on Polygon.&nbsp;</p><h3 id="will-there-be-a-polymarket-airdrop">Will there be a Polymarket airdrop?</h3><p>There is frequent speculation about whether Polymarket will launch a token or hold an airdrop.</p><p>As of now, the team has not officially confirmed plans. However, speaking on a podcast, <a href="https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2025/10/24/polymarket-will-launch-token-and-airdrop-after-u-s-relaunch-cmo-says"><u>Polymarket CMO said</u></a>, “There will be a token, there will be an airdrop”. But the plans are not imminent.&nbsp;</p><p>If a token ever launches, early and active users could benefit. But nothing is guaranteed.&nbsp;</p><p>Still, even without a Polymarket airdrop, you can already make money by trading events. </p><h2 id="how-to-get-started-on-polymarket">How to get started on Polymarket</h2><p>To begin trading on Polymarket, you need a <a href="https://zerion.io/polygon-wallet" rel="noreferrer">Polygon wallet</a> and USDC on Polygon.&nbsp;</p><p><a href="https://link.zerion.io/4SO8fUbZrYb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion Wallet</a> simplifies onboarding by helping you bridge funds, connect instantly, and track all your predictions and manage them in a single mobile app.&nbsp;</p><p>If you don’t have any USDC on Polygon, bridge it with Zerion:&nbsp;</p><ol><li>Open the bridge tab in <a href="https://link.zerion.io/4SO8fUbZrYb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion</a></li><li>Select the network and chain from which to send assets&nbsp;</li><li>Select Polygon as the chain and USDC as the token to receive&nbsp;</li><li>Sign the transaction&nbsp;</li></ol><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2025/11/Bridge-USDC-to-Polygon.png" class="kg-image" alt="Bridge USDC to Polygon in Zerion" loading="lazy" width="1355" height="990" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/2025/11/Bridge-USDC-to-Polygon.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1000/2025/11/Bridge-USDC-to-Polygon.png 1000w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2025/11/Bridge-USDC-to-Polygon.png 1355w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"><figcaption><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">Bridge USDC to Polygon in Zerion</span></figcaption></figure><p>That’s it.
