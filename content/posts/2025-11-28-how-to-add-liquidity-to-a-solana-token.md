<p>Adding liquidity on Solana isn’t just for advanced DeFi users anymore. With today’s tools, anyone can support their favorite token, earn fees, or set up a gradual selling strategy. In this guide, you’ll learn exactly how to add liquidity to a Solana token, what to expect when providing liquidity, and how to manage your LP positions directly in Zerion.</p><h2 id="steps-to-provide-liquidity-on-pumpswap">Steps to Provide Liquidity on Pumpswap</h2><p>While there are many <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/best-solana-dexes-a-comprehensive-guide/" rel="noreferrer">Solana DEXes</a>, we’ll use Pumpswap, the DEX by Pump.fun to illustrate the process of adding liquidity. These steps are the same on other DEXes but the interface might be slightly different.&nbsp;</p><ol><li>Open <a href="https://zerion.io/solana-wallet" rel="noreferrer">Zerion Wallet for Solana</a> and navigate to the built-in browser&nbsp;</li><li>Enter <a href="https://swap.pump.fun/"><u>https://swap.pump.fun/</u></a> in the URL field&nbsp;</li><li>Connect your wallet&nbsp;</li><li>Tap [deposit/create]</li><li>Select the tokens pair for which you want to provide liquidity&nbsp;</li><li>Sign the transactions&nbsp;</li></ol><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2025/11/Add-liquidity-on-Solana.png" class="kg-image" alt="Adding tokens to a Pumpswap liquidity pool in Zerion's mobile browser" loading="lazy" width="1355" height="990" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/2025/11/Add-liquidity-on-Solana.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1000/2025/11/Add-liquidity-on-Solana.png 1000w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2025/11/Add-liquidity-on-Solana.png 1355w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"><figcaption><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">Adding tokens to a Pumpswap liquidity pool in Zerion's mobile browser</span></figcaption></figure><p>Now you’re a liquidity provider for this token and will earn trading fees.&nbsp;</p><h2 id="how-to-manage-solana-lp-positions">How to Manage Solana LP Positions</h2><p>After providing liquidity, your LP tokens stay in your wallet. You can see them by connecting to Pumpswap and tapping [positions].&nbsp;</p><p>When you want to remove liquidity, simply return to Pumpswap, connect your Zerion Wallet, and choose [deposit/create] and then ‘withdraw’. You’ll sign a couple of transactions, and your tokens will be returned to your wallet instantly.</p><h2 id="why-provide-liquidity-to-a-solana-token">Why Provide Liquidity to a Solana Token</h2><p>People provide liquidity for several reasons.&nbsp;</p><h3 id="earn-lp-fees">Earn LP fees</h3><p>Liquidity providers earn a portion of the trading fees generated by the liquidity pool. The more activity and volume the token gets, the more fees your LP position can accumulate. It’s a simple way to put idle assets to work.&nbsp;</p><h3 id="support-the-token">Support the token&nbsp;</h3><p>Nobody can buy a token if there is no liquidity. Creators must provide liquidity to the pool for others to buy.&nbsp;</p><p>Other holders who have lots of tokens might also choose to provide liquidity to help the token grow by making it more accessible to buyers.&nbsp;</p><h3 id="gradually-sell-the-token">Gradually sell the token</h3><p>Providing liquidity can also act as a smoother selling strategy.&nbsp;</p><p>Instead of market-selling large amounts at once, LPing allows you to exit gradually as traders interact with the pool. This often gives a better average execution price, puts less impact on price, and even earns trading fees in the process.&nbsp;</p><h2 id="conclusion">Conclusion</h2><p>Adding liquidity to a Solana token is a straightforward process: pick a platform like Pumpswap, connect Zerion Wallet, choose your token pair, and sign the transactions. Once your LP tokens are in your wallet, Zerion makes it easy to track the value, monitor fees, and manage your position over time.</p><p>If you want the smoothest way to interact with Solana dapps and track all your tokens, install <a href="https://zerion.io/solana-wallet" rel="noreferrer">Zerion Wallet for Solana</a> and start exploring.</p>
