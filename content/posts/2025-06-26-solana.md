<p>The wait is over. <a href="https://zerion.io/solana-wallet" rel="noreferrer">Solana wallet</a> is now available on Zerion. From memecoins to DeFi, you can grow your crypto portfolio across Solana, Ethereum, and 50+ EVM chains—all in one app. Available on the web, as a mobile app, and as a browser extension.&nbsp;</p><p>Here’s what’s new.&nbsp;</p><h2 id="swap-in-a-tap-at-the-best-rates">Swap in a tap at the best rates</h2><p>Like on other major chains, you can swap any token on Solana right in Zerion.&nbsp;</p><p>Under the hood, Zerion Wallet integrates Jupiter to find the best rate for any token. It’s faster than ever: no need to open the browser and navigate the dapp.&nbsp;</p><h2 id="bridge-to-solana-in-seconds-from-50-chains">Bridge to Solana in seconds from 50+ chains</h2><p>To get started on Solana, you can bridge from any supported EVM chains. Zerion will find the cheapest and fastest route for your bridging transaction.</p><h2 id="track-your-portfolio-across-solana-and-ethereum-ecosystems">Track your portfolio across Solana and Ethereum ecosystems</h2><p>You can import or add as ‘watched’ your existing Solana addresses.&nbsp;</p><p>By adding them to Zerion along with your Ethereum addresses, you can automatically calculate the value of your entire portfolio. No more adding rows in spreadsheets!</p><h2 id="follow-other-wallets-and-copy-their-moves">Follow other wallets and copy their moves</h2><p>You can explore any Solana wallet in Zerion. Just enter an address in the search field and you’ll see everything the wallet holds and all its past trades.&nbsp;</p><p>This is a great way to learn from the best traders.
