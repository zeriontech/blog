<h4 id="securely-manage-your-defi-portfolio-on-the-go">Securely manage your DeFi portfolio on the go</h4><p>We recently added <a href="https://shop.ledger.com/pages/ledger-nano-x" rel="noopener">Ledger Nano X</a> to the list of wallets supported on our <a href="https://link.zerion.io/ZRpP9R4mdEb" rel="noreferrer">iOS mobile app</a>.</p><p>Ledger’s most advanced hardware wallet is Bluetooth compatible, which means you can securely trade DeFi assets on the go. With over 50 DeFi protocols supported on our mobile app, you’ll have access to every DeFi strategy — including liquidity pools, vaults, indexes and automated strategies, and indexes.</p><p><em>Note: This integration is currently only available on iOS, but the Android integration is on our short-term roadmap.</em></p><h3 id="securely-trade-on-the-go"><strong>Securely trade on the go</strong></h3><p>Zerion’s <a href="https://blog.zerion.io/zerion-the-decentralized-coinbase-c35a92cde729" rel="noopener">optimized trade routing</a> sources liquidity from all major decentralized exchanges, offering you the best rates and lowest gas costs, with zero added fees. Connecting your Ledger Nano X is as easy as opening the Zerion app on <a href="https://apps.apple.com/us/app/zerion-invest-in-defi/id1456732565?utm_medium=social&amp;utm_source=twitter&amp;utm_campaign=ledger_nano_x&amp;utm_content=external&amp;utm_term=ios" rel="noopener">iOS</a> and following these steps:</p><ol><li>Connect your wallet</li><li>Connect your Ledger Nano X device via Bluetooth</li><li>Connect your Ethereum wallet address</li></ol><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/800/1-b31hiO4ynbDLRrXWEFF4aQ.png" class="kg-image" alt="Connect your Ledger Nano X to Zerion" loading="lazy"></figure><p>Once your Ledger Nano X is connected, select an asset, input the amount you wish to buy, and you’ll be prompted on your device to confirm the transaction.
