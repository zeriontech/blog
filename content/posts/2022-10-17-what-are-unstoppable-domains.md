<p>If your desired .eth handle is no longer available, do not despair. There are many other NFT domains out there.</p><p>In this post, you’ll find out what Unstoppable Domains are, how they work, how much they cost, and how to use them.</p><h2 id="what-are-unstoppable-domains">What are Unstoppable Domains</h2><p>Unstoppable Domains are domain names that are NFTs on Ethereum and the <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/what-is-polygon-matic-and-how-to-use-it-with-zerion-wallet/">Polygon network</a>.</p><p>The use of NFTs, makes these domain names decentralized and <em>unstoppable. </em>Traditional domain names like .com are entries in databases of centralized registries, which makes them susceptible to censorship. <a href="https://app.zerion.io/collections/unstoppable-domains">NFT domains like Unstoppable</a> are ERC-721 tokens owned by individual addresses.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2022/10/Screenshot-2022-10-17-at-15.07.56.png" class="kg-image" alt="Unstoppable Domains examples" loading="lazy" width="2000" height="663" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/2022/10/Screenshot-2022-10-17-at-15.07.56.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1000/2022/10/Screenshot-2022-10-17-at-15.07.56.png 1000w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1600/2022/10/Screenshot-2022-10-17-at-15.07.56.png 1600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2022/10/Screenshot-2022-10-17-at-15.07.56.png 2130w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"><figcaption>Some of the Unstoppable Domains that were recently sold on OpenSea</figcaption></figure><p>Unstoppable Domains offers several top-level domains, including:</p><ul><li>.crypto</li><li>.nft,</li><li>.wallet,</li><li>.x,</li><li>.dao,</li><li>.bitcoin,</li><li>.888,</li><li>.blockchain,</li><li>.zil</li></ul><p>Once you mint an Unstoppable Domain, you own it forever (or until you sell/transfer it). There are no renewal fees.</p><h2 id="how-do-unstoppable-domains-work">How do Unstoppable Domains work</h2><p>You can mint and hold an Unstoppable Domain in your wallet like any other NFT.</p><p>Under the hood, Unstoppable Domains are built on CNS, the Crypto Name Service. This serves the same purpose as the traditional DNS, i.e. looking up the address of a domain owner. However, CNS is based on smart contracts and domain ownership is irrevocable. Once a domain is minted, nobody can change, deactivate or transfer the domain without its owner’s permission.</p><p>Not all browsers currently support NFT domains. Here is a list of browsers that already support Unstoppable Domains’ CNS:</p><ul><li>Opera for desktop and mobile (supports .crypto)</li><li>Brave for desktop and mobile (supports .crypto)</li><li>Google Chrome Extension (supports all Unstoppable Domains)</li><li>Firefox Extension (supports all Unstoppable Domains)</li><li>Unstoppable Browser (supports .crypto and .zil)</li></ul><p>With an Unstoppable Domain and IFPS for hosting, you can build a decentralized website that will be displayed in supported browsers without any risk of censorship.</p><h2 id="unstoppable-domains-vs-ens-domains">Unstoppable Domains vs ENS domains</h2><p>Both Unstoppable Domains and ENS or Ethereum Name System are implementations of NFT domains.</p><p>The main difference is that <a href="https://app.zerion.io/collections/ens">ENS domains NFTs</a> must be renewed annually while Unstoppable Domains have no renewals.
