<h4 id="we-just-became-your-all-in-one-defi-shop">We just became your all-in-one DeFi shop.</h4><p>Decentralized finance has come a long way. Remember your first transaction? After juggling 5+ tabs on your browser, you gazed at etherscan’s mint green block confirmation feeling like you’d just contributed to the future of finance. You felt so cool, you even told a friend about it — convinced them to finally break up with Coinbase and trade on the wild side.</p><p>Except your friend quickly got lost. And so did you. Instead of the “easy, open finance” you were both promised, you found yourselves in a world of gas prices, pending transactions, vaults, pools, AMMs, degens, and financial advice spewed out in 17-thread Tweetstorms by Chads and sushi chefs.</p><blockquote><strong>centralized exchanges felt a lot safer and simpler</strong><em>made sense</em></blockquote><p>We know that most users have had little choice when it comes to how they access crypto, which is why centralized exchanges have survived for so long. But what if we told you things have changed in a <strong>big</strong> way? What if you could complete every step of the <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/how-to-invest-in-defi/">DeFi</a> journey — from exploring new assets and placing a trade, to investing in complex primitives and tracking your portfolio — all from one place?</p><p>We’ve integrated every major DeFi protocol and liquidity provider, which means <strong>your favourite portfolio tracker just became the easiest and cheapest place to invest in DeFi.</strong></p><h3 id="stacking-the-defi-journey-into-one-experience"><strong>Stacking the DeFi journey into one experience</strong></h3><p>Whether you’re a DeFi native, finally making the jump from centralized crypto exchanges, or transferring directly from traditional finance, Zerion is now the one place that supports all of these actions:</p><ol><li>Exploring new assets</li><li>Single-transaction investments</li><li>Accurate portfolio tracking</li></ol><h4 id="exploring-new-assets"><strong>Exploring new assets</strong></h4><p><em>Coin Gecko + DeFi Market Cap + Portfolio = Zerion</em></p><p>Keeping up with the market has never been easy, which is why we built the Explore and Market pages.
