<p><strong>Hello frens.</strong> In April, the Zerion team shipped some new NFT features and several other updates. Just add their addresses to watched wallets, turn on push notifications, and get alerts every time they mint an NFT.</p><p>Zerion’s new NFT layout now also lets you sort NFTs by price, collection, and the time they were added.
