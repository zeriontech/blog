<p>We’re excited to announce that we’ve just added <a href="https://www.wintermute.com/" rel="noopener">Wintermute</a> as a liquidity provider on Zerion. As a leading global algorithmic market maker, Wintermute offers minimal slippage, low gas costs and deep liquidity from centralized exchanges.</p><p>The quote comparison feature on our web and mobile apps allows you to see exactly how much you’re paying for a trade, and why. Wintermute will now appear under the “Zerion” tag in the list of available liquidity sources.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-embed-card"><blockquote class="twitter-tweet"><a href="https://twitter.com/zerion_io/status/1388163859261706243"></a></blockquote><script async="" src="https://platform.twitter.com/widgets.js" charset="utf-8"></script></figure><h3 id="why-wintermute">Why Wintermute?</h3><p>Wintermute operates as a market maker for centralized and decentralized exchanges. They support over 3,000 pairs and see an average of $6 billion traded per day from 4–5 million transactions.</p><p>In the <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/how-to-invest-in-defi/">DeFi</a> world, Wintermute is quickly becoming a key player with a proven record for providing deep liquidity when it matters most — they’ve helped bootstrap several DEXs and have assisted in the smooth execution of <a href="https://twitter.com/sandeepnailwal/status/1393269044611010567" rel="noopener">large-scale Covid relief efforts</a>.</p><blockquote>“Wintermute is thrilled to be working together with the Zerion team! We believe that an all-in-one front-end interface for DeFi is the best way forward for retail adoption.” — Yuriy Myronovych, Head of DeFi at Wintermute</blockquote><p>Wintermute promises</p><ul><li>access to broader liquidity, including centralized exchanges,</li><li>minimal slippage from leveraging multiple pools of liquidity, and</li><li>lower gas costs.</li></ul><p><a href="https://medium.com/dragonfly-research/unbundling-uniswap-the-future-of-on-chain-market-making-1c7d6948d570">On-chain liquidity is evolving fast</a>. With this integration, we’re leveraging a centralized liquidity source to make decentralized investing a better experience for everyone.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/800/1-I567GdEAQ-vWXV_Trqf1aQ.png" class="kg-image" alt="Wintermute" loading="lazy"><figcaption>Wintermute quote for WBTC/USD pair on Paraswap, showing lower slippage than most decentralized exchanges.</figcaption></figure><hr>
