<h4 id="zerion-is-the-world%E2%80%99s-simplest-interface-to-invest-in-defi-%E2%80%94-learn-how-to-do-so-on-the-go-with-our-android-and-ios-apps">Zerion is the world’s simplest interface to invest in DeFi — learn how to do so on the go with our Android and iOS apps</h4><p>If you’re completely new to DeFi, you may want to check out our tutorial on how to create a DeFi portfolio. In this article, we discuss how to connect your <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/how-to-invest-in-defi/" rel="noopener">DeFi</a> portfolio to your mobile phone so you can track your assets on the go.</p><p><strong>Step 1:</strong> Download the Zerion app for Android or iOS</p><p><strong>Step 2:</strong> Import your <a href="https://link.zerion.io/SUhN3URmdEb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion wallet</a> or manually input your <a href="https://zerion.io/ethereum-wallet" rel="noreferrer">Ethereum wallet</a> address. If you import your Zerion Web wallet, you’ll need to scan the QR code that can be found under “QR code” on the web interface.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/800/1-PEKIWTd2OKN-eNNYuzjO8A.png" class="kg-image" alt="Scan crypto wallet on Zerion" loading="lazy" width="800" height="435" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/max/800/1-PEKIWTd2OKN-eNNYuzjO8A.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/800/1-PEKIWTd2OKN-eNNYuzjO8A.png 800w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"></figure><p><strong>Step3:</strong> You can now add as many read-only addresses as you like, including wallets that are not your own!</p><p>For example, check out our template Bankless portfolios geared towards different types of investors:</p><ul><li><strong>stable.zerion.eth:</strong> a low-risk, fixed income style portfolio with 60% of assets in stablecoin bets and 40% in Uniswap Pools</li><li><strong>bankless.zerion.eth:</strong> heavy exposure to DeFi money protocols such as ETH, BTC, MKR and USDC</li><li><strong>defimax.zerion.eth:</strong> a portfolio that bets on the growth of DeFi money protocols and high volume Uniswap liquidity pools</li></ul><p><strong>Step 4:</strong> Use the Zerion mobile app to get familiar with DeFi: track the performance of other portfolios, view your complete transaction history and check out the Explore tab to see trending liquidity pools, the best DeFi rates, and more!</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/800/1-35KozA00_BoqqyYRtWMqhA.png" class="kg-image" alt="Access DeFi on mobile with Zerion’s app" loading="lazy" width="800" height="454" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/max/800/1-35KozA00_BoqqyYRtWMqhA.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/800/1-35KozA00_BoqqyYRtWMqhA.png 800w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"></figure><hr>
