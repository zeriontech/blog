<p>We’re excited to announce that you can now provide liquidity to PieDAO’s DOUGH/ETH and Play/DOUGH Sushiswap pools and stake their LP tokens directly on Zerion!</p><p>Staking is one of the easiest ways to earn passive income as a DeFi investor.</p><p>Users who provide liquidity to these PieDAO pools and stake through our interface are eligible to earn DOUGH, which lies at the heart of PieDAO’s ecosystem of automated wealth creation.</p><h3 id="what-is-piedao">What is PieDAO?</h3><p>PieDAO is a decentralized asset manager with a focus on automated wealth creation. They introduced their “Pie” indexes to the DeFi community for the first time in March 2020, offering diversified portfolios of top-performing crypto assets with active yield-generating strategies</p><p><strong>Pies</strong> stand out from other <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/defi-indexes-explained/">DeFi indexes</a> due to their baked-in yield farming strategies that maximize returns with minimal effort. In addition, Pie holders enjoy significantly lower minting costs thanks to PieDAO’s community “Oven” which can save up to 97% in <a href="https://help.zerion.io/en/articles/4057577-what-are-gas-fees" rel="noopener">gas fees</a>.</p><h3 id="stake-to-earn-dough-rewards">Stake to earn DOUGH rewards</h3><p>Anyone who stakes the DOUGH/ETH and Play/DOUGH Sushiswap pools on Zerion is eligible to earn DOUGH, the governance token behind PieDAO. DOUGH is used for PieDAO governance and, and active members are rewarded with a share of the DAO’s profits.</p><p>PieDAO recently launched their DOUGHpamine incentive program to boost liquidity provision for DOUGH &amp; PIEs, therefore making it easier for users to build positions without slippage.</p><h3 id="staking-on-zerion">Staking on Zerion</h3><ol><li>Connect your <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/custodial-vs-non-custodial-wallet-wallets-comparison-zerion-io/">non-custodial wallet</a> on <a href="https://app.zerion.io" rel="noopener">app.zerion.io</a>. If you’re starting from scratch, check out our Help Center to get set up.</li><li>Head over to the DOUGH/ETH or Play/DOUGH liquidity pool pages on Zerion</li><li>Supply liquidity to the pool by using the widget next to the asset chart</li><li>Once you hold liquidity pool shares, you’ll be able to stake them directly on the asset page</li><li>Track your staked positions on the Overview page</li><li>You can claim your rewards at any time by clicking “Claim” on the liquidity pool asset page</li></ol><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/800/1-70RpBA9c1_IQy33fX8LSeQ.png" class="kg-image" alt="Sushi DOUGH/ETH liquidity pool" loading="lazy" width="800" height="444" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/max/800/1-70RpBA9c1_IQy33fX8LSeQ.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/800/1-70RpBA9c1_IQy33fX8LSeQ.png 800w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"></figure><hr><p>Got questions? Reach out to anyone on the Zerion or PieDAO team.</p>
