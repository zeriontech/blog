<p>The mainnet of Abstract Chain, one of the most anticipated L2 networks, is live! And from day one, it’s fully supported by Zerion. In this post, we’ll explore Abstract and show you how to get started.&nbsp;</p><h2 id="what-is-abstract-chain">What is Abstract Chain?&nbsp;&nbsp;</h2><p>Launched team behind Pudgy Penguins, <a href="https://abs.xyz/"><u>Abstract Chain</u></a> is an Ethereum Layer-2 network for consumer apps.&nbsp;</p><p>Build with <a href="https://zkstack.io/"><u>ZK Stack</u></a>, Abstract is an EVM-compatible zk-rollup. This means it functions almost exactly like Ethereum yet unlocks new functionality while offering drastically lower fees and faster transactions.&nbsp;</p><p>Abstract is also part of ZKSync’s Elastic Chain, which counts Zerion’s <a href="https://zero.network/"><u>ZERO Network</u></a>, <a href="http://lens.xyz"><u>Lens</u></a>, and other chains as members. All these networks will eventually share liquidity, users, dapps, and more.&nbsp;</p><p>Now let’s see how Abstract works.&nbsp;</p><h2 id="how-abstract-chain-works">How Abstract Chain Works&nbsp;</h2><p>From the user’s point of view, Abstract can work like a regular Ethereum L2.&nbsp;</p><p>Or the chain can be abstracted (hence the name!) from the user experience. Consumer apps could use onchain transactions under the hood while offering smooth UX with native account abstraction and paymasters.&nbsp;</p><p>Besides traditional self-custodial wallets like Zerion Wallet, developers can also use Abstract Global Wallet (AGW).&nbsp;</p><p>AGW is a set of smart contracts to onboard users with familiar login methods (email, socials, passkeys and more). Users don’t need to worry about seed phrases or even leave the app. That’s the future.&nbsp;</p><p>In the present, you can use Abstract with <a href="https://link.zerion.io/j0iluX2ILQb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion Wallet</a>.&nbsp;</p><h2 id="abstract-chain-in-zerion">Abstract Chain in Zerion&nbsp;</h2><p>With Zerion, you can be both a <em>consumer</em> and <em>creator</em> on Abstract.&nbsp;</p><h3 id="track-abstract-chain-in-zerion-wallet">Track Abstract Chain in Zerion Wallet&nbsp;</h3><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2025/02/abtract.jpeg" class="kg-image" alt="" loading="lazy" width="2000" height="1125" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/2025/02/abtract.jpeg 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1000/2025/02/abtract.jpeg 1000w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1600/2025/02/abtract.jpeg 1600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2025/02/abtract.jpeg 2400w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"></figure><p>Once you bridge ETH or other tokens to Abstract Chain, they will automatically show up in your <a href="https://link.zerion.io/j0iluX2ILQb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion Wallet</a>. No need to add any settings manually.&nbsp;</p><p>After that, you’ll be able to use Abstract just like other chains:&nbsp;</p><ul><li>Swap right in Zerion — in the <a href="https://app.zerion.io/swap?chainInput=abstract" rel="noreferrer">Zerion swaps in the web app</a> or on mobile &nbsp;</li><li>Track all your tokens and NFTs&nbsp;</li><li>See your transaction history&nbsp;</li><li>Follow other wallets and get notifications when they do something cool on Abstract (great way to get alpha!)&nbsp;</li></ul><p>All this Abstract data is also available to builders.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><h3 id="build-on-abstract-chain-with-zerion-api">Build on Abstract Chain with Zerion API&nbsp;</h3><p><a href="https://zerion.io/api"><u>Zerion API</u></a> offers full support for the Abstract chain from day one across all <a href="https://developers.zerion.io/reference/endpoints-and-schema-details"><u>API endpoints</u></a>.</p><p>You can use Zerion's <a href="https://zerion.io/api/abstract" rel="noreferrer">Abstract API</a> to easily add onchain data to any consumer products even without any knowledge of web3:&nbsp;</p><ul><li>Get tokens and NFTs held by an address&nbsp;</li><li>Get multichain transaction history for any address&nbsp;&nbsp;</li><li>Get the value of the overall portfolio&nbsp;</li><li>And more!&nbsp;</li></ul><p><a href="https://dashboard.zerion.io/?utm_source=abstract-blog&amp;typeform-source=zerion.io" rel="noreferrer"><u>Get a free dev key</u></a> and see Zerion API in action!&nbsp;</p><h2 id="what%E2%80%99s-next">What’s Next&nbsp;</h2><p>In this post, we explored what Abstract Chain is and how you can get started on with <a href="https://link.zerion.io/j0iluX2ILQb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion Wallet</a>.&nbsp;</p><p>We are excited to welcome Abstract to the wider Elasctic Chain community.
