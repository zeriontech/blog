<h2 id="put-your-crypto-to-work-on-protocols-like-compound-balancer-synthetix-and-curve">Put your crypto to work on protocols like Compound, Balancer, Synthetix and Curve</h2><p>Yield Farming is all the rage these days.</p><p>Whether it be supplying assets to Compound, providing liquidity on Balancer, or taking part in Synthetix’s dozens of liquidity incentives, it’s a good time for honest work.</p><p>In this article, we’ll walk through the recent trends and how Zerion is helping farmers keep tabs on their seeds, ultimately creating a unified experience to deploy, monitor, and harvest the fruits of their labor in a few clicks.</p><h1 id="what-is-yield-farming"><strong>What is Yield Farming?</strong></h1><p>For those who haven’t been on Twitter in the past week, Yield Farming is a meme that represents users putting their capital to work to earn returns on an endless number of different <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/how-to-invest-in-defi/">DeFi</a> protocols.</p><p>Best catalyzed by <a href="https://compound.finance/" rel="noopener ugc nofollow">Compound</a> and the distribution of their governance token — COMP — users who supply or borrow capital from the leading lending protocol are eligible to receive a pro-rata distribution of the 2,880 COMP allocated each day.</p><p>This led to over <a href="https://defipulse.com/compound" rel="noopener ugc nofollow">$1B in new capital</a> flowing into the protocol, with obscure tokens like BAT seeing upwards of 20,000% increases in demand as farmers pivoted to farm COMP. Paired with the ability to maximize COMP by using Flash Loans on InstaDapp, Compound’s token distribution has solidified a DeFi bull market. Here’s a tutorial from <a href="https://twitter.com/DeFi_Dad" rel="noopener ugc nofollow">DeFi Dad</a> on how it works:</p><figure class="kg-card kg-embed-card"><iframe width="200" height="113" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/lcLqPLRFfro?start=4&amp;feature=oembed" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" title="How to Maximize Earning $COMP By More Than 5X Using Instadapp"></iframe></figure><p>Given the scarce supply of COMP, the price quickly surged from its listing value of $16/token to a high of $400 when it was listed on Coinbase Pro earlier this week. While this is definitely an extreme example, there are many reasons why COMP is now the<a href="https://defimarketcap.io/token/0xc00e94cb662c3520282e6f5717214004a7f26888" rel="noopener ugc nofollow"> #1 token on DeFiMarketCap</a>.</p><p>Using Zerion, users can actively monitor the value of COMP, along with entering the protocol using the Save feature and claiming their tokens directly through the dashboard.</p><h1 id="bal-liquidity-mining"><strong>BAL Liquidity Mining</strong></h1><p>Before there was COMP farming, there was BAL mining.</p><p>This week, <a href="https://balancer.finance/" rel="noopener ugc nofollow">Balancer’s</a> governance token — BAL — is making big waves thanks to the distribution of its <a href="https://medium.com/balancer-protocol/balancer-liquidity-mining-begins-6e65932eaea9" rel="noopener">Liquidity Mining program</a> which launched three weeks ago. Just like with COMP, BAL tokens are distributed for the usage of the protocol — in this case for supplying capital to any of the 427 Balancer Pools.</p><p>With a base price of $0.60/token, BAL has quickly surged to over $15, giving it the <a href="https://defimarketcap.io/token/0xba100000625a3754423978a60c9317c58a424e3d" rel="noopener ugc nofollow">#2 slot on DeFiMarketCap</a>.</p><p>What’s interesting to note here is that the Balancer community has actively reshaped how BAL is earned using a variety of factors. From fees to weights and wrapping, Balancer has shown strong promise for the ability to prioritize value-added liquidity by modifying their token distribution.</p><p>Now, less than a week after launch, Balancer has seen over <a href="https://defipulse.com/balancer" rel="noopener ugc nofollow">$30M in new capital</a> enter the asset management protocol along with over 270% more unique liquidity providers and more liquidity than UniswapV2.</p><p><em>Notice a trend yet?</em></p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2022/07/0_ZJfv6hMjTC94vXxT.png" class="kg-image" alt="" loading="lazy" width="1258" height="272" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/2022/07/0_ZJfv6hMjTC94vXxT.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1000/2022/07/0_ZJfv6hMjTC94vXxT.png 1000w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2022/07/0_ZJfv6hMjTC94vXxT.png 1258w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"></figure><h1 id="why-yield-farming-works"><strong>Why Yield Farming Works</strong></h1><p>Looking at yield farming from a purely token perspective, the mechanism of introducing tokens to the open market in an extremely conservative fashion is <strong>rocket fuel.</strong></p><blockquote>In the case of both COMP and BAL, the only circulating tokens are those distributed through yield farming and those owned by the team and investors.</blockquote><p>Seeing as we know the team and investors have a vested long-term interest, this basically leaves yield farmers left to trade amongst one another.</p><p>To paint a clear picture, here’s how tokens enter the market from the aforementioned projects.</p><ul><li>COMP — 2,880 COMP per day or 1M (1%) per year</li><li>BAL — 145,000 BAL per week or 7.54M (7.54%) per year</li></ul><p>What emerges is a vast amount of hungry tokenholders fighting for an extremely limited supply of tokens. Plus, with the added benefit of interest in the case of Compound and swap fees in the case of BAL, the <a href="http://www.predictions.exchange/" rel="noopener ugc nofollow">Return on Liquidity</a> (ROL) is two-fold.</p><p>When factoring in the price of these tokens, there are exponential returns to be earned for trading in harvested governance tokens.</p><h1 id="og-yield-farming"><strong>OG Yield Farming</strong></h1><p>Going back further, we have <a href="https://www.curve.fi/" rel="noopener ugc nofollow">Curve</a>.</p><p>Curve is a liquidity aggregator that offers low-slippage trades between pegged assets like stablecoins and different wrappers of Ethereum-based Bitcoin.</p><p>This past week, Curve experienced its ATH in volume multiple days in a row, largely due to so many people rushing to get USDT from leveraged Maker DAI to farm COMP.
