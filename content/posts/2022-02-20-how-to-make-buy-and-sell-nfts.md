NFTs are also becoming popular among crypto enterprises to reward loyal customers and promote particular actions.</p><p>Recent headlines have established NFTs as a leading use case within the larger crypto movement, with eye-popping sales figures from marketplace Opensea and endorsements from celebrities like <a href="https://www.theblockcrypto.com/post/109655/jay-z-puts-a-cryptopunk-nft-as-his-twitter-profile-picture">Jay Z</a>.</p><p><strong>But, exactly, what are NFTs?</strong></p><p>In a nutshell, an NFT is a one-of-a-kind digital asset with its ownership recorded on the blockchain. This prevents it from being copied, reproduced, or otherwise duplicated. As a result, the value of NFTs is linked to the concept of scarcity. The fewer of anything there is, the more precious it is. </p><p>The majority of NFT projects are now developed on the Ethereum blockchain. Unlike bitcoin, Ethereum has advanced smart contract capabilities that enable the development of NFTs with unique attributes. Newer blockchains like Solana are also rolling out their NFT ecosystems.</p><p>On Ethereum, users can purchase and trade NFTs with ETH or other ERC20 tokens like stable coins.</p><p>Let's go into everything you need to know about making, selling, and collecting NFTs now that we've covered the basics.</p><h2 id="what-kinds-of-nfts-are-there">What Kinds of NFTs Are There?</h2><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2022/03/Screenshot-2022-03-18-at-13.28.46.png" class="kg-image" alt="" loading="lazy" width="2000" height="608" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/2022/03/Screenshot-2022-03-18-at-13.28.46.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1000/2022/03/Screenshot-2022-03-18-at-13.28.46.png 1000w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1600/2022/03/Screenshot-2022-03-18-at-13.28.46.png 1600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2022/03/Screenshot-2022-03-18-at-13.28.46.png 2000w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"><figcaption><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">NFTs range from art and music to domain names and virtual world items</span></figcaption></figure><p>Although it's easy to lump all NFT projects into one category, the reality is that the metaverse's NFT initiatives are almost as diverse as the collecting and gaming markets in the "real" world.</p><p>Examples of NFT types include:</p><ol><li><strong>Art</strong></li></ol><p>Beeple's $69 million dramatic sales at Christie's auction house last year launched NFTs into the art world. The holder of an NFT (or a group of NFTs) would get ownership of a proportional fraction of the real-world asset.</p><p>Although it is far from the only example, real estate tokenization is likely the easiest use case to get your head around.
