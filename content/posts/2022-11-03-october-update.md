<p>gm Zer,</p><p>Did you see that <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/the-next-step-to-building-the-best-wallet/">Zerion raised $12.3m Series B</a>? The goal is to build the best wallet for Web3 citizens. </p><p>Our mission from the very beginning has been to empower YOU — Web3 builders, traders, and enthusiasts — with user-friendly and intelligent products that help you take control of your identities, data &amp; assets. </p><p>Now, this vision attracted the Wintermute team and other world-class partners to join our mission.</p><p>You can read more about the state of Zerion and future plans in <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/the-next-step-to-building-the-best-wallet/">the blog post</a> and on <a href="https://twitter.com/zerion_io/status/1580170512021344256">Twitter</a>.</p><h2 id="it%E2%80%99s-in-your-dna">It’s in your DNA</h2><p>It pays to hold a Zerion DNA and evolve with every transaction. </p><p>First, the DNA unlocked a Lens handle, now Unstoppable Domains credits.  </p><p>Similar to ENS, <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/what-are-unstoppable-domains/">Unstoppable Domains</a> are NFT domain names. You can use them to host decentralized websites with IFPS or just as human-readable handles for your wallet address instead of a random string that starts with 0x. UD's Senior Vice President Sandy Carter also shared lots of other <a href="https://twitter.com/zerion_io/status/1580751684745457664">UD use cases on Zerion’s Web3 Wednesdays</a>.</p><p>In Zerion, you can now search an address by Unstoppable Domain and use it to send crypto, just like with ENS or Lens:</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2022/11/Screenshot-2022-11-02-at-14.01.37.png" class="kg-image" alt="Seach an Ethereum address by Unstoppable Domain with Zerion" loading="lazy" width="970" height="346" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/2022/11/Screenshot-2022-11-02-at-14.01.37.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2022/11/Screenshot-2022-11-02-at-14.01.37.png 970w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"><figcaption>Seach an Ethereum address by Unstoppable Domain with Zerion</figcaption></figure><p>And to celebrate this integration, <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/is-it-in-your-zerion-dna-get-50-unstoppable-domains-credit/">Unstoppable Domains gave $50 credits</a> to holders of Zerion DNA with at least Level 2 traits. </p><p>If you missed that, don't despair. We’re planning more cool stuff for <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/zerion-dna/">Zerion DNA</a> holders. You can prepare for that by leveling up your Zerion DNA. To do that you need to (1) hold Zerion DNA in a <a href="https://link.zerion.io/BQdwjgtuUtb">Zerion Wallet</a> and (2) do some transactions, ideally trade with Zerion swap. </p><p>Did you know that you can also <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/how-to-import-your-metamask-into-zerion-wallet/">import your MetaMask into Zerion Wallet</a>?</p><p>After that, you can mint (or transfer) Zerion DNA and see it change as you make transactions.</p><h2 id="learning-with-zerion">Learning with Zerion</h2><p>In October, we welcomed some amazing guests to Web3 Wednesdays, Zerion’s own Twitter Space. </p><p>Among them was Jbeezy from Lido. He spoke about the future of <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/how-to-stake-eth-lido-vs-rocket-pool-vs-stakewise/">liquid staking</a> on Ethereum, explained how Lido’s ETH staking works, and share lots of alpha. </p><p>We also heard from some of the most prominent builders of <a href="https://twitter.com/zerion_io/status/1582707793437806593">Regenerative Finance aka ReFi</a>. It’s all about building on top of DeFi to fund public goods, price in negative externalities, and generally make the world a better place. Johnx from Toucan Protocol, Ben West from GitCoin, and Douglas Qian from Spirals Protocol tuned in to share their stories.</p><p>Twitter isn’t the only place where live Zerion hangouts are happening. In our <a href="https://zerion.io/discord">Discord</a>, we host Zerion Connect calls — recently we heard from Ceres DAO about Web3 portfolio management. </p><p>To make sure you don’t miss any Zerion events, add the <a href="https://go.zerion.io/calendar">Zerion community calendar</a> to your calendar app.</p><h2 id="what%E2%80%99s-next">What’s next</h2><p>This week, some Zers are in Lisbon, Portugal, and San Francisco.
