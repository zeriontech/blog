<p><strong>Hello Zers. </strong>Even though August is often the popular time for vacations, the Zerion team was busy and we have quite a few updates for you. Read on to learn about new utility for Genesis NFTs, better multichain connectivity, wallet import, transaction review, and more.</p><h2 id="spotlight-another-step-towards-decentralization">Spotlight: <strong>Another step towards decentralization</strong></h2><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2022/09/spotlight-decentralization-nft-image.png" class="kg-image" alt loading="lazy" width="800" height="485" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/2022/09/spotlight-decentralization-nft-image.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2022/09/spotlight-decentralization-nft-image.png 800w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"></figure><p>Now, Zerion fee of 0.5% is applied on swaps for all chains including the Ethereum network. However, if you hold the Zerion Genesis NFT, you can trade with 0% Zerion fees. And if you don’t have it, you can buy a Zerion Genesis right in the web app — holding that will be the key for future premium features. <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/another-step-towards-decentralized-future-genesis-cards/" rel="noreferrer noopener">Learn more</a>. </p><hr><h2 id="highlights">Highlights</h2><p><strong>Connect to several dapps on any network. </strong>We added support for <a href="https://explorer.walletconnect.com/registry?type=hybrid&amp;version=2" rel="noreferrer noopener">WalletConnect v2</a>, enabling simultaneous multi-chain capabilities, one-time pairing for multiple sessions, and a wallet API permissions system.</p><p><strong>Import wallets with ease.</strong> We improved the steps for importing wallets to Zerion (<a href="https://twitter.com/zerion_io/status/1559582541165629446?s=20" rel="noreferrer noopener">watch the video</a>). This includes updating the text, tooltips, copy/paste button, QR scan, and sorting out the active wallets from the inactive ones.</p><p><strong>Intuitively review transactions.</strong> We redesigned the swap, bridge, and transfer interface with a sleeker. The more intuitive design lets you easily review and process crypto transactions.</p><p><strong>Wallet avatars. </strong>They now<strong> </strong>default to your Zerion DNA or the most valuable NFT owned in the wallet if you don’t have a Zerion DNA. Set up your NFT avatar now (<a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-Qt1_djpGOg" rel="noreferrer noopener">watch the video</a>).</p><p><strong>Better trading on Aurora.</strong> Added support for 1inch on the Aurora network to give you even better swap rates.</p><p><strong>Stay up to speed with the space.</strong> In August, we explored the biggest stories in Web3: the <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/nomad-bridge-hack/" rel="noreferrer noopener">$190m Nomad hack</a>, <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/tornado-cash-sanctioned-by-ofac-what-does-this-mean-for-defi/" rel="noreferrer noopener">Tornado Cash sanctions</a>, and <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/the-merge/" rel="noreferrer noopener">the Merge</a>.</p><hr><h2 id="community-love">Community love</h2><p><strong>Dots integration in our Discord. </strong>The new bot has different welcome flows for devs, beta-testers, and Zerion Wallet users.</p><p><strong>Interested in Zerion API?</strong> Quite a few people have asked about our upcoming REST API. Now you can <a href="https://zerion-io.typeform.com/to/wTY30GPv" rel="noreferrer noopener">sign up via this form </a>to request an API key for when it's live.</p><p><strong>Web3 Wednesdays.</strong> In our regular Twitter Spaces we explored <a href="https://twitter.com/zerion_io/status/1554447557664788480" rel="noreferrer noopener">Wallet Connect</a> with its founder Pedro and <a href="https://twitter.com/zerion_io/status/1554447557664788480" rel="noreferrer noopener">Metaverse Headquarters</a>, a tokenized community of NFT traders, investors, and educators.</p><p><strong>Zerion Connect.</strong> These community calls in our Discord will now be bi-weekly to give you even better content. The next one is on September 15, we'll celebrate the Merge!</p><p><strong>Zerion Community Calendar.</strong> You can track all Zerion calls and conferences in a single calendar: <a href="https://go.zerion.io/calendar" rel="noreferrer noopener">https://go.zerion.io/calendar</a></p>
