<p>In June 2023, during the depths of the bear market, we opened early access to our groundbreaking product — our browser extension wallet.</p><p>Since then, we’ve been steadily improving our extension thanks to the invaluable feedback from around 30,000 users from Zerion’s existing NFT holders and members of specific communities across web3 who tested the extension first.</p><p>Today we’re excited to announce that it’s finally ready, and&nbsp;<a href="https://link.zerion.io/YmqdGumd6Eb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion’s extension</a>&nbsp;is now available for everyone!</p><h2 id="this-is-not-%E2%80%98just-another%E2%80%99-extension">This is not ‘<em>just another’</em>&nbsp;extension</h2><p>With this release, our wallet is now publicly available as a browser extension for Chrome, Arc, and Brave.</p><blockquote>You may be thinking,&nbsp;<em>“Why launch another extension?”</em></blockquote><p>Since 2016, we've been at the forefront of the Ethereum ecosystem, gathering wisdom, insights, and expertise. Now, we're funneling all that knowledge into this wallet, meticulously designed and engineered to change your whole experience on the new internet.</p><p>It isn’t just another extension. It's your gateway to your onchain history, DeFi positions, NFT collections, and the most popular Layer-2s.</p><p>Today, Zerion is the easiest and <a href="https://zerion.io/security" rel="noreferrer">safest</a> way to explore every layer of this new internet.</p><h3 id="easiest-multichain-experience">Easiest multichain experience</h3><p>OGs in this space will remember that Zerion started out as a DeFi aggregator.</p><p>Early on, we recognized that just like the explosion in ERC-20 tokens, the growth of DeFi would lead to thousands of protocols. We set out to track them all.</p><p>We see something similar happening with dapps and other non-DeFi use cases today. And most of this expansion is not happening on Ethereum but on Layer-2s. Mirror is on Optimism. Lens is on Polygon. Friend.tech is on Base.</p><p>Adding new networks, switching between them, and understanding your complete onchain footprint is clunky and slow with most legacy wallets.</p><p>The&nbsp;<a href="https://link.zerion.io/YmqdGumd6Eb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion extension</a>&nbsp;makes all of this easier and clearer than ever.</p>

<p>All of these onchain interactions also generate so much data that it can be difficult to know&nbsp;<em>exactly</em>&nbsp;what you’ve done onchain. Zerion shines in bringing all of this information together in a human-readable format.</p><h3 id="understanding-your-onchain-footprint">Understanding your onchain footprint</h3><p>You can’t be an owner unless you know what you own and where you’ve been.</p><p><a href="https://link.zerion.io/YmqdGumd6Eb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion’s extension</a>&nbsp;makes it easy to see all your onchain stuff, including tokens, DeFi positions, NFTs, and transaction history — across all supported EVM networks.</p><p>While blockchain data is open to all, making sense of it isn’t as simple as it should be. This is one reason why the majority of wallets don't even try to display staked tokens, NFTs, or other complicated assets.</p><p>But understanding your onchain footprint is crucial for anyone in this space.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://zerion.mirror.xyz/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.mirror-media.xyz%2Fpublication-images%2FU-wdRgwjHnJD5-hnmfTta.png&amp;w=3840&amp;q=75" class="kg-image" alt="Track and manage your positions right in the extension." loading="lazy" width="2530" height="1608"><figcaption><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">Track and manage your positions right in the extension.</span></figcaption></figure><p>With Zerion, you’ll see all your assets and transaction history across 14 chains on our mobile apps, browser extension, and web app — consistently and in the same familiar format. The goal is to give you everything you need to stay in control and make informed choices.</p><h3 id="the-safest-wallet-for-self-custodial-humans">The safest wallet for self-custodial humans</h3><p>Getting the right information can also help you stay safe in Web3.</p><p>The default degen way is to ape, sign, and see what happens. This approach was profitable during the early days when crypto was a smaller space.</p><p>Now sophisticated scammers pray on these habits. Not just newcomers but OGs lose money by acting on FOMO and blindly signing messages.</p><p>Zerion Wallet leverages data to give you actionable information to fight these scams:</p><ol><li>Filter out onchain spam so you don’t even see most scams in your wallet</li><li>Warn you if you <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/crypto-phishing-what-it-is-and-how-zerion-can-help-to-protect-you/" rel="noreferrer">try to connect your wallet to a malicious dapp</a></li><li>Show you the transaction’s result before you sign so you can dodge wallet drainers</li></ol>
