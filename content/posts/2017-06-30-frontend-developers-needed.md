<blockquote>Zerion is a rapidly-expanding fintech company with an expertise in creating blockchain-based applications. We have experience in smart contract development on Ethereum blockchain and our focus is on helping companies raise funds through ICOs.</blockquote><h4 id="job-description"><strong>Job description</strong></h4><p>We are looking for talented <strong>frontend</strong> <strong>developers</strong> who are proficient in building complex, clean front-ends in <strong>React</strong>.</p><p>You will be working on the best-in-class product for <em>cryptocurrency investments</em> that will help companies handle <em>multimillion dollar</em> investment rounds.</p><p>Your primary focus will be on developing user interface components interacting with APIs, communicating with blockchain nodes and managing local users’ accounts.</p><p>Our development team consists of more than 10 people. Your work will require coordination with other engineers and management on product decisions.</p><hr><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p><ul><li>Help our to team to develop a large, user-facing service using <em>React.js</em></li><li>Build <em>reusable</em> components</li><li>Translate designs and wireframes into high-quality code</li><li>Optimize components for maximum performance across <em>devices and browsers</em></li><li>Implement custom systems based on asymmetric key cryptography</li></ul><p><strong>Requirements</strong></p><ul><li>Passion for blockchain, smart contracts and the future of finance :)</li><li>Strong proficiency in JavaScript.</li><li>Thorough understanding of React.js and its core principles</li><li>Experience with popular React.js workflows (such as Flux or Redux)</li><li>Familiarity with RESTful APIs</li><li>Familiarity with Git, Github, and code review process</li><li>Experience with common front-end development tools like Babel, Webpack, NPM, etc.</li><li>Ability to understand business requirements and translate them into technical requirements</li></ul><p><strong>Nice to have:</strong></p><ul><li>Understanding of blockchain, smart contracts, etc is a plus.</li><li>Knowledge of OAuth2 authentication process is a plus.</li><li>Familiarity with Ethereum’s Web3.js library.</li><li>Experience with unit testing JavaScript code</li><li>Experience in managing a team of frontend developers is a plus.</li></ul><p>Reach out to <a href="mailto:inbox@zerion.io">inbox@zerion.io</a> with your resumes or contact me on <a href="https://www.facebook.com/eugene.yurtaev.9" rel="noopener">Facebook</a>!</p>
