<p>Remember when the ‘Connect Wallet’ button was to replace dozens of different passwords? It was supposed to be a better alternative to the oligopoly of sign-in with Google, Facebook, and Apple. Well, it didn’t quite work. Until now.</p><h2 id="the-problem">The problem</h2><p>Today, you cannot connect <em>most</em> wallets to most dapps.</p><p>We <a href="https://zerion.mirror.xyz/vJC46dja5So4oGHCEU_J-MGGswuAnzSZHgvIx_V6TkQ" rel="noreferrer">tested 100 top dapps</a> and found that the ‘Connect Wallet’ button is clearly broken. Every third dapp gives you limited wallet options. Only 4% of dapps have 10+ login options. And 43% cannot detect multiple wallets.</p><p>Only 13% of dapps can automatically detect which wallet you have and let you connect.&nbsp;</p><figure class="kg-card kg-embed-card"><blockquote class="twitter-tweet"><p lang="en" dir="ltr">The Great Wall in&nbsp;Web3<br><br>I tried to use the top 100 dApps with three different wallets. Here’s the tea:☕️<br><br>Thread 🧵 <a href="https://t.co/oAZheYTm4I">pic.twitter.com/oAZheYTm4I</a></p>— Booga (@boogaav) <a href="https://twitter.com/boogaav/status/1714690022887293106?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">October 18, 2023</a></blockquote>
<script async="" src="https://platform.twitter.com/widgets.js" charset="utf-8"></script></figure><p>As a result, we risk recreating the same limited, permissioned structure of Web 2.0, where the user is forced to choose between just two or three options.</p><h2 id="the-solution-eip-6963">The solution: EIP-6963</h2><p>Developers from WalletConnect, Zerion, Brave, Rainbow, Wagmi, WalletConnect, and others created a simple way to discover which wallet the user has — <a href="https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-6963"><u>EIP-6963</u></a>.&nbsp;</p><p>EIP-6963 standardizes the way dapps and wallets exchange information to create a user-friendly wallet selection.&nbsp;</p><p>So when you click ‘Connect Wallet’, the dapp automatically recognizes which wallets you have and shows you only those options.&nbsp;</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2024/03/JMdUIwaG6y5JlkSROfTnK.png" class="kg-image" alt="" loading="lazy" width="1502" height="480" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/2024/03/JMdUIwaG6y5JlkSROfTnK.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1000/2024/03/JMdUIwaG6y5JlkSROfTnK.png 1000w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2024/03/JMdUIwaG6y5JlkSROfTnK.png 1502w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"></figure><p>It’s a win-win for everybody:&nbsp;</p><ul><li>Dapps show only the relevant wallets and don’t exclude any users</li><li>Wallets connect smoothly to dapps&nbsp;</li><li>People enjoy the freedom to use any wallet&nbsp;</li></ul><p>However, like any standard, EIP-6963 needs to gain wide adoption.&nbsp;</p><h2 id="who-is-already-in">Who is already in</h2><p>Luckily, some of the major dapps and wallets have already implemented EIP-6963:</p><ul><li>Zora, Uniswap, Daylight, Safe, Brave, CowSwap, Zapper, Rainbow, Zerion, Privy, Yearn, Bebop, Origin, WalletConnect, Frontier, TokenPocket, XDEFI.&nbsp;</li></ul><p>And this is just the beginning. To spread further, EIP-6963 requires your help.&nbsp;</p><p>That’s because dapp developers are busy and need to see that this is a priority, that we, the users, really need this.</p><h2 id="take-action-unblock-crypto">Take action: Unblock Crypto!</h2><p>To help spread the word and encourage dapps to implement the new standard, Zerion and partners are launching the <strong><u>Unblock Crypto</u></strong> campaign.</p><p>First, <a href="https://zora.co/collect/zora:0xbcf871fa7f85cae1218d2a47f121accf81830225/1" rel="noreferrer"><strong>mint the EIP-6963</strong></a> as an NFT on Zora.
