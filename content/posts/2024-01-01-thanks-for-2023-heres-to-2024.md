<p>The way out of the bear market is through. Buidl. And that’s what we did in 2023.&nbsp;</p><p>On the one hand, the year zipped past in a heartbeat. <h2 id="zerion-wallet-as-a-browser-extension">Zerion Wallet as a browser extension</h2><p>The biggest change was the arrival of the Zerion browser extension. It took exactly a year to create it completely from scratch to public release.&nbsp;</p><h3 id="early-access-campaign">Early access campaign&nbsp;</h3><p>Zerion extension first became available via the <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/zerion-extension-how-to-get-access/"><u>early access campaign</u></a>. Only holders of certain NFTs and members of web3 communities could get it.&nbsp;</p><p>Still, over 30,000 people installed the extension.
