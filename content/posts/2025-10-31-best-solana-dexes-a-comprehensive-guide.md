<p>Solana has become the home for decentralized trading, processing billions in daily volume across dozens of DEXes. This guide breaks down what makes Solana DEXes unique, compares the top platforms, and shows you how to trade efficiently using Zerion Wallet.</p><h2 id="what-is-a-dex">What is a DEX?</h2><p>A DEX, short for decentralized exchange, is a cryptocurrency exchange that lets you trade directly from your wallet without first sending crypto to a centralized platform.&nbsp;</p><p>Any DEX is a set of smart contracts that govern how tokens are traded. You can interact with these smart contracts via a DEX website or directly through a <a href="https://zerion.io/solana-wallet"><u>Solana wallet</u></a> that integrates them.&nbsp;</p><p>Here’s what sets DEXes apart from centralized exchanges like Coinbase or Binance:&nbsp;</p><ul><li><strong>Non-custodial: </strong>You make trades directly from your <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/custodial-vs-non-custodial-wallet-wallets-comparison-zerion-io/"><u>self-custodial wallet</u></a></li><li><strong>Transparent: </strong>All transactions are recorded on the blockchain, open to everybody&nbsp;</li><li><strong>P2P: </strong>You trade directly with other participants, either via orders or through a liquidity pool&nbsp;</li></ul><p>Solana DEXes are decentralized exchanges on the Solana blockchain.&nbsp;</p><h2 id="what-makes-solana-dexes-unique">What Makes Solana DEXes Unique</h2><p>Solana blockchain's architecture transforms how DEXes work compared to other chains.</p><ul><li><strong>Faster transaction: </strong>Solana can process up to 65,000 transactions per second, making it much faster than Ethereum and most other EVMV chains.&nbsp;</li><li><strong>No token approvals: </strong>Unlike on Ethereum, you don’t need first to approve the token to be used by a DEX. This means you can make a trade much faster.&nbsp;</li><li><strong>Ultra-low fees:</strong> Fees on Solana are negligible compared to Ethereum. You can make a lot more trades without worrying about fees eating into your profits.&nbsp;</li><li><strong>Innovative mechanisms</strong>: Solana’s programs enable <a href="https://solana.com/learn/exploring-solana-applications#decentralized-exchanges-dexs"><u>new applications</u></a> that were not possible on older blockchains.&nbsp;</li></ul><p>But most components are the same across DEXes on Solana and Ethereum, where the first decentralized exchanges originated:&nbsp;</p><ul><li><strong>Automated Market Maker</strong> models, where liquidity providers deposit token pairs into pools. Traders swap directly with these pools using mathematical formulas to determine pricing.</li><li><strong>Liquidity pools</strong> hold token pairs, and the balance of tokens determines the price.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li><li><strong>Aggregators</strong> can route trades via different pools to get the best price.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li></ul><h2 id="top-solana-dexes">Top Solana DEXes</h2><p>Let's break down the leading platforms and what makes each one unique.</p><h3 id="raydium">Raydium</h3><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2025/10/Screenshot-2025-10-31-at-16.43.28.png" class="kg-image" alt="Raydium DEX" loading="lazy" width="2000" height="1001" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/2025/10/Screenshot-2025-10-31-at-16.43.28.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1000/2025/10/Screenshot-2025-10-31-at-16.43.28.png 1000w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1600/2025/10/Screenshot-2025-10-31-at-16.43.28.png 1600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w2400/2025/10/Screenshot-2025-10-31-at-16.43.28.png 2400w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"><figcaption><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">Raydium: the OG Solana DEX</span></figcaption></figure><p>Raydium pioneered automated market making on Solana back in 2021.
