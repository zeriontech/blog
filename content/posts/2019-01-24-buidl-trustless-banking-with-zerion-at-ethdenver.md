<p>Hey ETHDenver hackers! We are excited to announce that Zerion will be attending ETHDenver this year, and we are looking forward to seeing what you will #buidl for the future of Decentralized Finance on top of Ethereum!</p><p>ETHDenver is free for everyone wanting to participate, so make sure to apply as their last round of applications closes January 31, 2019.</p><p>Last year marked a very important milestone for Ethereum. Thanks to the tireless work of hackers like you, #HODL was replaced by #BUIDL, and we saw an emergence of decentralized open-source financial primitives like tokens (ERC20 &amp; ERC721), decentralized exchanges to trade them (0x, Kyber Network, Uniswap), stablecoins (MakerDAO), money markets (Compound) and even marginal trading (DyDx, bZx). All the above created a Decentralized Finance movement (DeFi), that is on track to provide the world with a more robust, transparent and global financial system.</p><p>Zerion is now building a trustless bank to unify the space of Decentralized Finance to foster usability and adoption of DeFi. We believe that the true value of open-source finance comes from the interoperability of these protocols. Therefore, we are looking for novel applications, use cases, or better implementations of DeFi protocols for future integration within Zerion.</p><h4 id="resources-to-start-building-for-defi"><strong>Resources to start #building for DeFi</strong></h4><ol><li>Intro to DeFi, stablecoins, decentralized exchanges on Zerion Blog</li><li>0x developer docs</li><li>Compound docs</li><li>MakerDAO docs</li><li>A curated list of existing DeFi projects on GitHub</li></ol><h4 id="meet-our-team-at-ethdenver"><strong>Meet our team at ETHDenver</strong></h4><p>Two members of our team will be joining you at ETHDenver, our CTO — Alex and DeFi protocol hacker Vadim!</p><p>Prior to the hackathon, feel free to join our Telegram group, or directly message our CTO, Alex!</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/800/1-qzf5Z_LodtKOLTbJTxNfPw.png" class="kg-image" alt="" loading="lazy" width="800" height="95" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/max/800/1-qzf5Z_LodtKOLTbJTxNfPw.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/800/1-qzf5Z_LodtKOLTbJTxNfPw.png 800w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"></figure><p>Check out our <a href="https://zerion.io" rel="noopener">website</a>, and follow us on Twitter!</p>
