<p>After months of hard work, I am proud to share that the ZERϴ Network mainnet is live!</p><p>ZERϴ Network will offer users a way to transact without paying any gas fees, overcoming one of the most obvious and frustrating barriers that users experience onchain.&nbsp;</p><p>Combined with ZERϴ Network, Zerion becomes far more than <em>just </em>a wallet by removing gas fees from the equation. Without gas to worry about, users can focus on what they want to <em>do</em> onchain instead of figuring out <em>how</em> to do it.</p><p>This gas free future is already here. Beginning today, <a href="https://link.zerion.io/LlQ1ifRwXNb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion Wallet</a> users will be able to trade, mint and bridge on ZERϴ without paying transaction fees. </p><h2 id="why-zer%CE%B8-network">Why ZERϴ Network?&nbsp;</h2><p>This milestone has been years in the making.&nbsp;</p><p>When we launched our first product, Zerion’s portfolio tracker, our goal was simple: help users understand and manage their crypto portfolios.</p><p>A desire to make crypto more accessible and easier to use has underpinned all our products:&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><ul><li>2019: Portfolio tracking app to view all tokens, DeFi, and NFTs</li><li>2022: Mobile wallet to swap, mint, and transact on the go&nbsp;</li><li>2023: Browser extension to make multichain crypto simple&nbsp;&nbsp;</li></ul><p>Our relentless focus on user experience has attracted more than 1.6M wallets over the last five years. One user recently told me, “Zerion is as good as a wallet can be."</p><p>Although hearing this was exciting and humbling, we realized that to succeed in our mission truly, we needed to go beyond the wallet to address the persistent user experience challenges that can only be solved by a layer 2 network.&nbsp;</p><p>With ZERϴ, we want to make gas fees a thing of the past.</p><h2 id="crypto-without-the-gassle">Crypto Without the Gassle&nbsp;</h2><p>We believe it should be free to transact onchain. Imagine asking users to pay for your AWS servers every time they use your app.</p><p>When you make transactions on ZERϴ in Zerion Wallet, you will not pay for gas at all. This is made possible by the combination of the native account abstraction provided by the ZK Stack and our own custom paymaster.</p><p>You can read more about how we do this in the docs.&nbsp;</p><p>However, gas fees have historically served an important role: preventing network abuse. While L2s lowered gas costs, the volume of spam increased. For example, as of November 2024, over <a href="https://dune.com/queries/4121513/6950926"><u>60% of swaps</u></a> on Uniswap on Base have a value under $1.</p><p>To truly solve the gas problem, we need to eliminate costs for real people while fighting spam.</p><p>Our solution is called <strong>Onchain DNA</strong>.</p><p>Onchain DNA removes transaction fees on ZERϴ for real people instead of rewarding bots and spammers. With this built-in Sybil defense, ZERϴ checks every address' past transaction history to see if it’s eligible for free transactions.&nbsp;</p><p>By ensuring that we prioritize the right users, ZERϴ becomes the only network inhabited by real people.&nbsp;</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2024/11/image.png" class="kg-image" alt="" loading="lazy" width="1920" height="1080" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/2024/11/image.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1000/2024/11/image.png 1000w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1600/2024/11/image.png 1600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2024/11/image.png 1920w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"><figcaption><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">Transaction flow on ZERϴ</span></figcaption></figure><h2 id="zer%CE%B8-friction">ZERϴ Friction</h2><p>We’re building the leading ecosystem for dapps, builders and protocols committed to creating a crypto experience with zero friction.</p><p>Today, you can get started on ZERϴ by bridging with Zerion.</p><p>To help us achieve this vision, Zerion has raised an exclusive fundraising round from a world-class group of angel investors, including Cooper Turley (CoopRecords), Spencer Noon (OurNetwork), Banteg, Alex Gluchowski, Patricio Worthalter (POAP), and Duncan Robinson (NBA player/ Miami Heat). Angels from Lido, Daylight, Celestia, ETHGlobal, Decent, and OpenSea also participated. </p><h2 id="a-new-beginning">A New Beginning</h2><p>Zero gas in <a href="https://link.zerion.io/LlQ1ifRwXNb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion Wallet</a> is only the first step to making transactions free for real people. Our vision is to make gas a thing of the past across all wallets and apps.&nbsp;</p><p>The launch of ZERϴ represents a new beginning for the Zerion team, and we can’t wait to show you what we have planned next.</p><p>Thank you for being a part of this journey!&nbsp;</p>
