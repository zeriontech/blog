<p>Staking AVAX can be a great way to earn a yield passively. However, the initial setup can be costly and complicated. Luckily, with BENQI and <a href="https://link.zerion.io/VnitLQIqSNb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion Wallet</a> you can stake AVAX in just a few clicks. In this post, you’ll learn AVAX staking, how it works, and how to get started.&nbsp;</p><h2 id="what-is-avax-staking">What is AVAX Staking?&nbsp;</h2><p><a href="https://zerion.io/blog/avalanche-week-on-zerion-heres-how-to-use-zerion-wallet-for-avalanche/" rel="noreferrer">Avalanche</a> is a proof-of-stake EVM Layer-1 blockchain. By staking AVAX tokens, you can help secure the blockchain and earn more AVAX as staking rewards.&nbsp;</p><p>The APY for staking AVAX is usually about 5-7%, fluctuating based on the supply of staked tokens within the network.&nbsp;</p><p>Avalanche has two networks:&nbsp;</p><ul><li><strong>The C-Chain</strong>, the EVM chain where DeFi and other smart contracts live&nbsp;</li><li><strong>The P-Chain</strong>, the non-EVM chain that co-ordinates validators&nbsp;</li></ul><p>AVAX staking happens on the P-Chain, where you must use a native Avalanche wallet.&nbsp;</p><p>If you have AVAX on C-Chain, you would usually need first to bridge it to P-Chain. Then you can stake. And if you want to withdraw AVAX and use it in DeFi, you would need to bridge back to C-Chain. All that can be costly and slow. Instead, you can use liquid staking.&nbsp;</p><h3 id="avax-liquid-staking">AVAX Liquid Staking&nbsp;</h3><p>Liquid staking is when you stake tokens in a protocol and get another transferable token, which represents your staked tokens. These are called liquid staked tokens (LST).&nbsp;</p><p>On Avalanche, liquid staking is particularly useful because it bridges the gap between the C-Chain and the P-Chain. You stake AVAX on C-Chain and get LST, which can be traded or used in DeFi. The protocol then stakes your AVAX on P-Chain and pays back the rewards to you on the C-Chain.&nbsp;</p><p>The leading liquid staking provider on Avalanche is BENQI.</p><h2 id="what-is-benqi">What is BENQI?&nbsp;</h2><p>Launched in 2021, <a href="https://benqi.fi/" rel="noreferrer">BENQI</a> is a suite of DeFi protocols on Avalanche with over $400 million in total value locked (TVL).&nbsp;</p><p>BENQI’s Liquid Staking protocol tokenizes staked AVAX as sAVAX, the LST you can move, trade, and use in DeFi.&nbsp;</p><p>Here is how liquid staking works at a high level:&nbsp;</p><ul><li>You stake AVAX with BENQI on the C-Chain, i.e. the EVM network with all the DeFi</li><li>BENQI gives you sAVAX and delegates AVAX to validator nodes on P-Chain and
