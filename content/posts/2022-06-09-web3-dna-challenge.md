<p><a href="https://zerion.io/dna/">Zerion DNA</a>, the first-of-its-kind living NFT, recently took Ethereum by storm.</p><p>In the first few hours after the NFT minting opened, Zerion DNA’s smart contract became the fourth biggest gas-guzzler in the world. During a 5 hour period, the only projects that generated more gas spending than Zerion DNA were omnipresent projects like USDT, Uniswap, and OpenSea. In less than two weeks, over 35,000 Zerion DNAs have been minted.</p><p>Unlike other NFTs, Zerion DNA’s rarity does not depend on luck. Instead, every on-chain action increases your DNA’s rarity. It welcomes both experienced builders and newbies alike. It’s also a place where you can meet all the Web3 legends.</p><p>Zerion will cover the winner’s flight, hotel, and, of course, the ticket to DevCon.</p><p>And if you can’t travel for whatever reason, we’ll send you the equivalent in ETH.</p><p>Excited?
