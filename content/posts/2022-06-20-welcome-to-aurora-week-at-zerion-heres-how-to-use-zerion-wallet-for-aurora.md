<p>Zerion Wallet, a non-custodial smart &amp; social wallet for DeFi and NFTs, now supports Aurora. In that case, the exchange manages your private key. There is a tradeoff with centralized services and your tokens are relatively siloed.</p><p>A non-custodial wallet for Aurora gives you (and <em>only</em> you) full control over your private key and assets that it stores. A smart wallet like <a href="https://link.zerion.io/6QMDHynR0qb">Zerion</a> lets you unlock everything that DeFi, NFTs, and Web3 offer:</p><ul><li>Earn a yield and rewards by staking in DeFi protocols on Aurora</li><li>Buy, sell, and display NFTs</li><li>Sign in and use any decentralized applications</li><li>Vote for governance proposals</li></ul><h2 id="how-can-i-create-an-aurora-wallet">How can I create an  Aurora Wallet?</h2><p>Because Aurora is compatible with Ethereum, you can generally use any <a href="https://zerion.io/ethereum-wallet" rel="noreferrer">Ethereum wallet</a> that allows you to add custom networks.
