<p>In March, we <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/preparing-for-a-decentralized-future-at-zerion/">introduced Zerion fees</a> for swaps on all networks and sidechains with the exception of Ethereum. These fees are then sent to treasury-like reserves in preparation for a decentralized future.</p><p>Starting August 29, we’re adding a Zerion fee for swaps on the Ethereum network.</p><p>Holders of a Genesis Card will not only have the <a href="https://link.zerion.io/cKUw7JWqSNb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion Wallet</a> fee waived for swaps on all chains but will also enjoy advanced trade routing.</p><p>Soon, we’re also bringing premium features to power our users with advanced DeFi &amp; NFT tracking and investing tools. The Genesis Card will act as an NFT membership, unlocking access to these features.</p><p>Before discussing what those features could be and how to get them, let’s briefly explain what’s driving this move.</p><h2 id="0-to-150000-in-5-months">0 to $150,000 in 5 months</h2><p>Over the past months, we saw that our users are willing to pay for a valuable service.</p><p>We introduced a 0.5% fee for <a href="https://link.zerion.io/cKUw7JWqSNb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion Wallet</a> swaps on Layer-2 and alternative Layer-1 networks. These fees went straight into multisig wallets. Funds are safely held there while we consider ways to involve the community in managing Zerion’s future.</p><p>This fee is still less than what many wallets and centralized exchanges charge for regular swaps.</p><p>Yet it generated over $125,000 for Zerion’s multisigs. You can see these funds in the multisig address for <a href="https://app.zerion.io/0x7d20ab6d8af50d87a5e8def46e48f4d7dc2ea5c7/overview">Avalanche and Optimism</a> as well as the one <a href="https://app.zerion.io/0x4a183b7ed67b9e14b3f45abfb2cf44ed22c29e54/overview">for all other networks</a>.</p><p>We also see that mainnet Ethereum is still the most popular network for Zerion users. So we’re adding a 0.8% fee to Zerion swaps on Ethereum. This will help to accelerate our path towards decentralization.</p><p>There is, however, a way to <strong>trade with 0% Zerion fees</strong>: buy and hold a <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/what-are-zerion-genesis-nfts-your-keys-to-free-easy-multichain-trading/">Zerion Genesis NFT</a> aka a Genesis Card, which acts as a fee waiver.
