<p>Web3 music is evolving into a full-blown narrative.</p><p>Yet, with many music NFT platforms and formats, it’s hard to stay on top of the market. That’s where <a href="https://spinamp.xyz/">Spinamp</a> comes in handy: it indexes and aggregates music NFTs, helping you explore and enjoy this growing space.</p><p>In this blog post, we’ll tell you what Spinamp is, how it works, and how you can use to build in the web3 music space.</p><h2 id="web3-music-landscape">Web3 music landscape</h2><p>Over the past few years, the web3 music space has evolved from a small niche in a sprawling ecosystem.</p><p>Unlike with other types of NFTs, OpenSea is not the dominant platform for music NFTs. Instead, many platforms emerged specifically to make it easy to <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/how-to-make-and-sell-music-nft/">create music NFTs</a>. While <a href="https://www.sound.xyz/">Sound.xyz</a>, <a href="https://catalog.works/">Catalog.works</a> are two of the best-known platforms, there are dozens of others.</p><p>Every day, new songs are minted. More and more artists join Web3 every day, attracted by immense possibilities and the freedom to interact directly with fans.</p><p>However, tracking and discovering songs and artists is hard.
