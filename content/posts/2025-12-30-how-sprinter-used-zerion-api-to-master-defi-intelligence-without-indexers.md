<p>When your business relies on analyzing DeFi credit and capital efficiency, "good enough" data doesn't cut it. You need to see the whole picture across every chain.</p><p>For Sprinter, a solver-powered liquidity protocol, this data is the engine behind their business development pipeline. But as a lean team, they faced a classic dilemma: burn engineering cycles building custom indexers or find a unified API that actually works.</p><p>Here is how Sprinter uses <a href="https://zerion.io/api" rel="noreferrer">Zerion API</a> to obtain institutional-grade protocol intelligence without the infrastructure burden.</p><h2 id="what-is-sprinter">What is Sprinter?&nbsp;</h2><p><a href="https://sprinter.tech/"><u>Sprinter</u></a> combines solving-as-a-service infrastructure with credit-based cross-chain liquidity. It has two main products:&nbsp;</p><ul><li><strong>Sprinter Stash</strong> — a credit protocol that connects LPs who deposit funds and solvers who can take out uncollateralized loans to execute their strategies,&nbsp;</li><li><strong>Sprinter Solve</strong> — an API that enables the execution of intent-based swaps.</li></ul><p>For these products, Sprinter has several different types of partners, including dapps, wallets, and protocols.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><h2 id="the-challenge-the-debt-blind-spot">The challenge: The debt blind spot</h2><p>Sprinter is based on rigorous research.</p><p>Before engaging with a partner, the team delves into onchain data, analyzes address behaviour, and simulates scenarios. They look at this data for all chains where they operate: Ethereum mainnet, OP, Base, and Arbitrum. They need to answer complex questions:</p><ul><li>Who are the power users on this protocol?</li><li>What are their deposits and outstanding borrowings?</li><li>How do these positions perform historically?</li></ul><p>The team tried several solutions but hit a wall: while most providers could see deposits, they couldn't get clear data on debts. Without liability data, accurately modeling credit risk is impossible.</p><p>The alternative was to build custom indexers for every protocol and every chain. That was a non-starter.&nbsp;</p><p><em>"I have zero interest in building and maintaining indexers. We have more critical jobs to do."</em> — Gregory Markou, Founder at Sprinter</p><h2 id="the-solution-one-api-for-the-full-balance-sheet">The solution: One API for the full balance sheet</h2><p>Sprinter turned to Zerion API’s <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/how-to-fetch-multichain-defi-positions-for-wallet-with-zerion-api/" rel="noreferrer">DeFi<strong> </strong>protocol positions</a> endpoint. Unlike other providers that only surface simple token balances, Zerion provided the complete picture Sprinter needed:</p><ol><li><strong>Full liability visibility:</strong> Sprinter could see outstanding debt alongside deposits, enabling accurate credit risk modeling.</li><li><strong>Mutilchain data:</strong> Sprinter could fetch data across all its chains with a single API call.&nbsp;</li><li><strong>Unified schema:</strong> Instead of wrestling with raw RPC calls, Sprinter pulls normalized, ready-to-use data directly into their analytics pipeline for further analysis.</li></ol><h2 id="the-impact-the-bd-superpower">The impact: The "BD superpower"</h2><p>Implementing Zerion API changed how Sprinter sells.</p><p>With its Zerion-powered dashboard, Sprinter gets more insight into the protocol’s user base than even the team they are pitching.</p><blockquote>"With Zerion, I can do a quick scan, walk into a BD meeting, and tell you all about your protocol. I probably have more info than most teams do on their own protocols." — <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/gregory-markou/" rel="noreferrer">Gregory Markou</a>, Founder at Sprinter</blockquote><h3 id="key-outcomes">Key outcomes</h3><ul><li><strong>Zero maintenance:</strong> Sprinter avoided the cost and headache of building chain-specific indexers.</li><li><strong>Faster deal cycles:</strong> Instant access to protocol usage allows for rapid qualification of BD opportunities.</li><li><strong>Better risk models:</strong> Complete data on borrowings and transaction history powers more accurate backtesting and pricing simulations.</li></ul><h2 id="what%E2%80%99s-next">What’s next</h2><p>Sprinter continues to scale its credit and capital modeling capabilities.&nbsp;</p><p>By using Zerion as their wallet data layer for onchain memory, they can focus on their core product, trusting Zerion to handle the complexity of multi-chain indexing.</p><p><a href="https://zerion-io.typeform.com/ZerionAPI?utm_source=blog&amp;utm_medium=article&amp;utm_campaign=sprinter-case-study" rel="noreferrer"><u>Get your free dev keys</u></a> and try Zerion API.</p>
