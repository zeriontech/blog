<h2 id="why-we-built-a-better-wallet">Why we built a better wallet</h2><p>The word “web3” has seeped into every industry over the past year. Artists, banks, creators, even governments want to own a piece of the future of the internet.</p><p>But as this user-owned cyberspace becomes a reality, we felt that one critical piece of infrastructure was lagging behind: <strong>wallets</strong></p><p>Your wallet is the gateway to your on-chain identity and sits at the heart of web3. Unfortunately, wallet management has consistently been the most painful aspect of onboarding users into this brave new world.</p><p>It’s time to get yourself a <strong>smart wallet</strong>.</p><h2 id="we-deserve-smarter-wallets">We deserve smarter wallets</h2><p>Let’s face it, we need better wallets that are built to scale alongside the warp speed innovation happening in web3. We’ve witnessed a decade of bad design around wallets and we’re here to change that. If your wallet isn’t mobile-friendly, chain agnostic and compatible with every dapp – we say ditch it. </p><blockquote>“Until now, it’s been impossible to have a smooth web3 experience from your mobile phone. The Zerion Wallet is the first web3 wallet that is entirely self-custodial, mobile-first, chain agnostic and compatible with any web3 app – and that’s why it’s a game changer.” <br><br>– Andrei Baliasnikov, Head of Product at Zerion. </blockquote><p>The Zerion Wallet includes:</p><ul><li>Smart trade routing across every major decentralized exchange</li><li>Multichain support for 10+ networks including <a href="https://zerion.io/ethereum-wallet" rel="noreferrer">Ethereum</a>, Polygon, Avalanche, Arbitrum, Binance Smart Chain, Optimism, Gnosis Chain and others.</li><li>Dapp compatibility via Wallet Connect. Sign transactions on any web3 app directly from your phone.</li><li>Fiat onramps to go from cash to crypto in just a few taps.</li><li>Advanced multi-seed phrase management – import any external wallet including MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Rainbow Wallet, and others.</li><li>Zerion DNA – the first generative NFT that evolves with you in web3</li></ul><h2 id="get-your-zerion-smart-wallet">Get your Zerion Smart Wallet</h2><ol><li>Download the Zerion mobile app on <a href="https://zerion.io/download" rel="noreferrer">iOS or Android</a></li><li>Follow the instructions on screen to either create a new wallet or import an existing one</li></ol><p>Once connected, you’ll have one place to manage every wallet, seed phrase and private key you own regardless of where you first created it. Use the wallet to place trades on any network, manage your multichain portfolio and store your NFTs. </p><p>Say goodbye to app-switching, importing token addresses and UI that belongs in the 2010s. Zerion becomes your mission control for <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/how-to-invest-in-defi/">DeFi</a> – unrivaled access blended into an exceptionally beautiful interface.</p>
