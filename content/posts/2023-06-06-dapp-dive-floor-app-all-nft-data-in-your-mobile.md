So Floor solves that problem: it has all the data you need and none of the noise you don’t need.</p><p>Although the Floor app is still gated, already over 10,000 users track over 22,000 wallet addresses.</p><h2 id="nft-data-in-your-pocket">NFT data in your pocket</h2><p>The Floor app lets you connect your wallet and tracks your entire NFT portfolio (including collections on Solana and
