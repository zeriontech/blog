So what looks like a promising position might become impossible to exit at the price you see on the screen.&nbsp;</p><h3 id="hype-cycles-move-fast">Hype cycles move fast&nbsp;</h3><p>A meme coin can go from launch to moon to forgotten in days or even hours. There is a small window to identify opportunities, enter positions, and take profits. This speed creates opportunity but demands discipline and quick decision-making.</p><p><strong>Factors that influence price:</strong></p><ul><li>Community growth and social media engagement</li><li>Celebrity or influencer mentions</li><li>Viral moments or cultural relevance</li><li>Token holder distribution (watch out for concentrated whale holdings)</li><li>Listing on centralized exchanges and perps platforms</li><li>Overall market sentiment toward Solana and crypto</li></ul><h3 id="risk-assessment-framework">Risk Assessment Framework</h3><p>Let's be brutally honest: trading Solana meme coins is high-risk.&nbsp;</p><p>Many traders lose money. But understanding the risks helps you trade smarter.</p><p><strong>Common risks you'll face:</strong></p><ul><li><strong>Rug pulls and scams.</strong> Meme coin creators or “devs” can remove additional liquidity or sell massive token allocations, leaving traders holding worthless coins. This happens frequently in the meme coin space.</li><li><strong>Extreme volatility.</strong> 50% swings in minutes aren't unusual. A profitable position can evaporate before you react.</li><li><strong>Low liquidity traps.</strong> You might be able to buy easily but find no buyers when you want to sell.</li><li><strong>FOMO-driven decisions.</strong> Seeing others make gains when the price goes up clouds judgment. Chasing pumps often leads to buying tops before prices crash.</li></ul><h3 id="how-to-identify-pump-and-dump-schemes">How to identify pump and dump schemes?&nbsp;</h3><p>Unfortunately, there is no guaranteed way, as scammers can be very sophisticated.
