That’s where cross-chain swaps and tools like Zerion come in.</p><p>Centralized solutions offer speed, but you have to trust a third party. Decentralized cross-chain solutions like bridges and DEXes keep you in control of your funds while letting you move assets wherever the market demands. Zerion aggregates offers from all bridges and DEXes, so you don’t need to figure out which one is fastest or cheapest. Zerion does it for you.</p><h3 id="how-fast-cross-chain-swaps-work">How Fast Cross-Chain Swaps Work</h3><p>Token bridges are the infrastructure that make cross-chain swaps possible. </p><p>Bridges lock your stablecoin on one network and release an equivalent on another. Modern bridges use smart contracts to handle this securely and transparently. Some, like atomic swaps, guarantee your trade is trustless: both sides are completed or nothing happens.</p><p>Liquidity pools add another layer of speed. If there’s deep liquidity on both sides, your transaction is more likely to be instant, and you avoid costly delays. </p><p>Zerion aggregates liquidity from all major bridges and DEXs, so your cross-chain swap happens with maximum speed and minimum slippage.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2025/07/Screenshot-2025-07-04-at-12.31.56.png" class="kg-image" alt="Example of bridging and swapping stablecoins in one go on Zerion" loading="lazy" width="813" height="701" srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/2025/07/Screenshot-2025-07-04-at-12.31.56.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/2025/07/Screenshot-2025-07-04-at-12.31.56.png 813w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"><figcaption><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">Example of bridging and swapping stablecoins in one go on Zerion</span></figcaption></figure><h3 id="what-affects-swap-speed">What Affects Swap Speed?</h3><p>Not all bridges and routes are equal. Transaction speed depends on:</p><ul><li>The specific bridging protocol (for example, <a href="https://bungee.exchange/" rel="noreferrer">Bungee</a>, <a href="https://stargate.finance/" rel="noreferrer">Stargate</a>, <a href="https://hop.exchange/" rel="noopener">Hop</a>, or <a href="https://www.connext.network/" rel="noopener">Connext</a>)</li><li>The level of network congestion on the source and destination blockchains</li><li>How much liquidity is available for your stablecoin on each side</li></ul><p>Atomic swaps and liquidity pools usually provide faster swaps, but some protocols are optimized for speed, while others focus on lowest fees or maximum decentralization. Zerion finds you cross-chain swap that are the fastest and cheapest.</p><h2 id="implementing-fast-cross-chain-stablecoin-swaps">Implementing Fast Cross-Chain Stablecoin Swaps</h2><p>When doing a cross-chain stablecoin swap, you essentially do two transactions: </p><ul><li>Swap from one stablecoin (e.g. USDC) to another (e.g. USDT)</li><li>Bridge the stablecoin from one chain (e.g. Base) to another network (e.g. mainnet Ethereum)</li></ul><p>The order of these transactions doesn't really matter but it can affect the fees you pay and the speed of execution. For example, swaps on Ethereum are more expensive and slow. <br><br>To remove the guesswork, you can use the <a href="https://app.zerion.io/" rel="noreferrer">Zerion</a> web app or <a href="https://link.zerion.io/PSwEbdkL3Wb" rel="noreferrer">Zerion Wallet</a> for this. </p><h3 id="choosing-the-best-bridge">Choosing the Best Bridge</h3><p>With Zerion, you don’t have to compare bridges yourself.
