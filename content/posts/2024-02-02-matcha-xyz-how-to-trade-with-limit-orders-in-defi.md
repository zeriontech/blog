It brings you the best price from across 100+ liquidity sources <em>and</em> lets you use <a href="https://matcha.xyz/limit-orders"><u>limit orders</u></a>. Just select the token you want to sell, the token to buy and get the best quote.&nbsp;</p><p>On Ethereum or Polygon, you can also turn on <a href="https://blog.matcha.xyz/article/matcha-auto"><u>Matcha Auto</u></a> for <a href="https://blog.matcha.xyz/article/mev-protection"><u>MEV protection</u></a>, gasless swaps, and optimal trade execution. Meanwhile, with limit orders you specify the limit price, i.e., the price you want to buy or sell.&nbsp;</p><p>If you expect the price to move, you can set limit orders and wait for them to fill. That’s a great way to buy dips even when you sleep. If the limit price isn’t hit, the limit order expires at its expiration date. Or you can cancel it manually at any time.</p><p>For limit orders, <a href="https://help.matcha.xyz/en/articles/4346691-how-are-limit-orders-on-matcha-free"><u>Matcha uses 0x</u></a>, which records orders off-chain and connects the buyer and the seller. The limit order is not submitted onchain, which means MEV bots can’t snipe your order.&nbsp;</p><p>Once the seller and buyer are matched, the trade is settled onchain.</p><h2 id="how-to-trade-with-limit-orders-on-matcha-dex">How to trade with limit orders on Matcha DEX</h2><p>It’s very easy to create a limit order on Matcha:</p><ol><li>Connect your Zerion wallet&nbsp;</li><li>Go to <a href="https://matcha.xyz"><u>Matcha</u></a> and switch to Limit order</li><li>Select the token you want to sell</li><li>Select the token you want to buy&nbsp;</li><li>Select the quantity you want —&nbsp;either how much you’re selling or how much you want to buy</li><li>Select the expiration date for your limit order</li><li>Click ‘Place order’</li><li>Sign the transaction in your Zerion wallet</li></ol><p>That’s it! The order is created. The buyer pays the settlement fee only once the transaction is submitted onchain.</p><p>You can create limit orders with Matcha to be settled on one of 3 chains: Ethereum mainnet, Polygon, and Binance Smart Chain.
