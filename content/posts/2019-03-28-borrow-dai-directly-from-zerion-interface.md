<h3 id="borrow-dai-directly-from-zerion">Borrow DAI directly from Zerion</h3><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/800/1-jES_wVQKU6OLo7zObXbuKg.png" class="kg-image" alt="Borrow DeFi assets like DAI from Zerion" loading="lazy"></figure><p>We are thrilled to announce the support for MakerDAO’s <em>collateralized debt positions (CDP)</em> in Zerion. Now, you are able to <strong>take out a loan in DAI</strong> backed by collateral in Ethereum.</p><h3 id="what-s-a-cdp">What’s a CDP?</h3><p>MakerDAO protocol allows you to take out loans denominated in DAI backed by collateral in Ether. Whenever you deposit your ETH into the system, a collateralized debt position (CDP) is created. You can then use it to mint DAI, a stable coin pegged to 1 $USD.</p><p>A loan in 🔸 DAI has two major use-cases:</p><ol><li>Transact at stable prices without selling your ETH</li><li>Use DAI to buy other tokens (you can do that in the “Exchange” tab)</li></ol><blockquote><a href="https://blog.zerion.io/defi-and-reliable-money-stablecoins-9d87098b495d" rel="noopener">in our blog</a></blockquote><h3 id="the-process-of-opening-a-cdp-with-zerion-is-easy-">The process of opening a CDP with Zerion is easy!</h3><p><strong>Step 1:</strong> Go to <a href="https://beta.zerion.io" rel="noopener">app.zerion.io</a> and navigate to the “Borrow” tab. <br>If you have opened a CDP before, you should see it here!</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/800/1-MoiAUw0TLBc2crAaJTACLw.png" class="kg-image" alt="Borrow DAI from Zerion" loading="lazy"></figure><p><strong>Step 2:</strong> Follow the instructions to open a new CDP.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/800/1-vlTRJS_33Tu9lsl3_EdwYw.png" class="kg-image" alt="Borrow DAI from Zerion" loading="lazy"></figure><p><strong>Step 3:</strong> Confirm the transaction in your selected wallet! <br>We currently support <a href="https://metamask.io/" rel="noopener">Metamask</a>, <a href="https://tokenary.io/" rel="noopener">Tokenary</a> and <a href="https://fortmatic.com/" rel="noopener">Fortmatic</a>.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-gallery-card kg-width-wide kg-card-hascaption"><div class="kg-gallery-container"><div class="kg-gallery-row"><div class="kg-gallery-image"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/400/1-h9J3KL6aREUuGcQ_WfhnfQ.png" width="712" height="1190" loading="lazy" alt srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/max/400/1-h9J3KL6aREUuGcQ_WfhnfQ.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/400/1-h9J3KL6aREUuGcQ_WfhnfQ.png 712w"></div><div class="kg-gallery-image"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/400/1-fNYBYePV3vl38GKchAN20A.png" width="1125" height="2436" loading="lazy" alt srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/max/400/1-fNYBYePV3vl38GKchAN20A.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w1000/max/400/1-fNYBYePV3vl38GKchAN20A.png 1000w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/400/1-fNYBYePV3vl38GKchAN20A.png 1125w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"></div><div class="kg-gallery-image"><img src="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/600/1-aV3V_jzJfpi91GOJML7kSA.png" width="616" height="812" loading="lazy" alt srcset="https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/size/w600/max/600/1-aV3V_jzJfpi91GOJML7kSA.png 600w, https://zerion.io/blog/content/images/max/600/1-aV3V_jzJfpi91GOJML7kSA.png 616w"></div></div></div><figcaption>MetaMask (left), Tokenary (middle), Fortmatic (right)</figcaption></figure><p><strong>Step 4:</strong> Congratulations! You have just created your first CDP :) <br>Now, you can manage your CDP from the “Borrow” tab.</p><p>The “Overview” tab lets you see the key parameters of your CDP. It shows your current debt, collateral, and their ratio.</p><blockquote>✅ As long as your debt does not surpass 66% of the collateral, your CDP remains safe, and you can take out more DAI if you need to.</blockquote><blockquote>⚠️ You will only be able to withdraw full collateral once you repay your full debt amount.</blockquote><blockquote>❗️ If your collateralization ratio rises above 66%, your CDP will be liquidated.</blockquote><h3 id="recap-what-you-can-do-with-zerion-already-">Recap: What you can do with Zerion already!</h3><ul><li>Track <strong>ETH</strong> and <strong>ERC20 </strong>tokens on<strong> </strong>as<strong> many wallets</strong> as you like</li><li>Watch the <strong>historical</strong> performance of your <strong>portfolio</strong></li><li><strong>Send</strong> and <strong>Receive</strong> crypto assets</li><li><strong>Buy</strong> and <strong>Sell</strong> more than 20 tokens</li><li><strong>Take out a loan</strong> in DAI backed with your ETH</li></ul><hr><p>📢 Follow us on <a href="https://twitter.com/zerion_io" rel="noopener">Twitter</a> to get latest updates, or join our <a href="https://zerion.io/discord">Discord</a> server.</p>
