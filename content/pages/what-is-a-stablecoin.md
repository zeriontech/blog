<p><em>Minimizing price volatility in DeFi</em></p><p><strong>💸 Stablecoin</strong></p><p>Stablecoins are designed to minimize price volatility by holding on to a specific value. There are generally three types:</p><ul><li><strong>Fiat-collateralized stablecoins</strong> e.g. Tether (USDT) and TrueUSD are pegged 1:1 to the US dollar and are backed by dollar deposits.</li><li><strong>Crypto-collateralized stablecoins</strong> e.g DAI — although this stablecoin is also pegged to the US dollar, its reserves are held in ETH</li><li><strong>Commodity-collateralized stablecoins</strong> e.g. Digix is a stablecoin backed by gold.</li><li><strong>Non-collateralized</strong> (algorithmic) stablecoins</li></ul><p>Learn more about <a href="https://zerion.io/blog/solana-stablecoins-the-complete-guide/" rel="noreferrer">Solana stablecoins</a>.</p>
